Starts   
Start the book strong. The disturbance can come later, but hook the read early.   
**Action** - in media res or with movement   
**Raw Emotion** - The character feeling an intense emotion   
**Look-Back Hook** - Lead is looking back at the inciting event   
**Attitude** - Strong character voice or opinion   
**Prologue**   
**Reader needs to bond with the main character.**   
Identification (empathy) with the lead   
1. trying to make it in the world   
2. a little fearful at times   
3. not perfect   
   
Sympathy   
1. Jeopardy - lead in danger   
2. Hardship   
3. Underdog   
4. Vulnerability

Inner Conflict   
Story World - what is life like for the lead?
