Mazda key programing   
Unlock the car   
Open drivers door, leave open, sit in car.   
With the lock switch on the door, lock the doors then unlock them.   
Put the Key in the ignition   
Turn the key to the on position (not just ACC) and back to&nbsp;Off&nbsp;3&nbsp;times within 10 seconds (if you have a manual it is easiest to just hold in the key release button for this whole process), leave the key in the ignition   
Close and open the&nbsp;drivers door 3&nbsp;times, leaving it open at the end.   
Your vehicle should lock and unlock the doors at this point   
Press any button on your first remote twice   
Your vehicle should respond again by locking/unlocking the doors   
Press any button on the new second remote twice   
Your vehicle should again lock and unlock the doors   
Repeat, if desired, for a 3rd remote.   
Last, take the key out of the ignition and your vehicle should lock/unlock the doors multiple times.   
Process complete
