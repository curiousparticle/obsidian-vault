General Novel Structure   
A disturbance and two doorways.   
The disturbance is the inciting incident. This is what disturbs the status quo. (e.g. Star Wars it is the message from Leia), once Luke sees that video, things start to happen, which propels him on his journey.   
Doorway 1, into 2, this is when hero decides to "Take Action" that leads him away from his current life. (Star Wars when Luke decides to go to space with Han, though it could be argued that he made that choice when his family's place was destroyed and he left with Ben)   
Doorway 2, Into 3, this is the start of end game. Things begin that causes the hero to move to the final confrontation. (Star Wars, when luck finds the rebels, joins and the death star appears and will destroy the base.)
