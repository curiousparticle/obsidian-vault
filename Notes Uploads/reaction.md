# Reaction   
Reaction   
Passive - character watches the scene and you get the scene.   
Reactive - character watches the scene and you the reaction to the action.   
This is the something is going on (pigs flying) but the view is on the character and what the character is thinking.   
Play And? So? to dig deeper into the character's reaction.
