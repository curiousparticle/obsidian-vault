Middles   
**Death**   
Can be psychological death, such as the death of innocence   
Or professional, such as getting fired   
**Opposition**   
Single person and more powerful that the lead.   
Adhesive - lead can't walk away, life and death, duty, obsession, physical location (Island).   
**ARM**   
Action, Reaction, More Action   
**Keep Them Reading**   
Stretching the Tension - don't speed through the tense parts, pace it.   
Raising the stakes   
**How to Energize a Lethargic Middle**   
Analyze the stakes   
Strengthen the adhesive   
Add more complication   
Add another character   
Add another subplot (don't over complicate)   
Push through (exhausted writer, don't give up)   
**How to Trim an Overweight Middle**   
Combine or cut characters   
Absorb a subplot   
Trim or combine scenes   
Trim the fat (the dull parts)
