Scenes   
Unforgettable scenes are fresh, surprising, and emotionally intense, about characters we care about doing things we *must* watch, using tension and originality.   
All scenes move the story forward.   
Four Cords of a Scene   
Major: action and reaction   
Minor: setup and deepening   
Scene - single location and continuous time   
Beat - an emotional/functional part of a scene.   
HIP   
- Hook the reader quickly with something interesting or shocking   
- Keep the intensity up, engaging the reader   
- Read on Prompts - End the scene memorably, wanting them to read the next scene   
   
**Tell** the **slow** bits   
**Show** the **exciting** bits.
