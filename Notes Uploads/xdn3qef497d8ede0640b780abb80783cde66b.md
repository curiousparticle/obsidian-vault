Dialogue and Monologue   
Understanding each characters perspective, desires, **how they speak** and how they see the world.   
Dialogue is somewhere between how people actually speak and the perfect. It is crafted for a purpose.   
- Illuminate Character   
- Advance the Story   
- Make Interesting Sentences   
   
Adverbs, use them very sparingly - useful for surprise, otherwise not.
