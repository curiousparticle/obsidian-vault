HC Elements of a Story   
**1 - Fish Out of Water**   
A character who is at odds with his/her environment of the concept itself.   
**2 - A Looming Conflict**   
A major problem/conflict that the audience instantly recognizes.   
**3 - A hook**   
The bait that lures people into the story - usually in the form of an aspirational, fearful, or escapism element.   
- Cool - Fresh and Unique.   
- Fish out of water can also be the hook.   
   
**4 - A Universal Theme**   
Heart and soul of the story   
- Love   
- Forgiveness   
- Acceptance   
- Faith   
- Courage   
- Trust   
- Survival   
- Confidence   
- Selflessness   
- Honor
