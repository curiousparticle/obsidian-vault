# Myrna   
Miria is cursed. She is not accepted in her own village and has to go over to the forest dwellers' village. There she is an outcast because she's cursed to look like the totem. But over time, she basically begins to realize that there's a lot she can give the village, and she finds her place even though she's an outcast. Then she realizes she can take that back to her own village.
