# Try more with Anytype   
Try out some additional features:   
  - **Import Content:** Go to Space Settings and select `Import` to bring in content from Notion or in markdown (from Obsidian & others) and additional formats   
  - **Explore the Gallery:** Click the `Gallery` icon next to your spaces on the left sidebar to get inspiration and download useful experiences   
  - **Create Types:** Click the `⋁` icon next to space name to add new types like tasks and bookmarks or create your own type from scratch   
  - **Add Widgets**: Click the `Add` button on the sidebar to customize your space even more   
We're excited for you to start your journey! Feel free to experiment and find what works best for you.
