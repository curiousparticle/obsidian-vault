## Shadow of the Beakman   
The tiny hamlet is under attack by a horde of beakmen: powerfully-built, green-skinned humanoids with fearsome beaks and strange, clawed hands. The beakmen are aided by croc knights: dread warriors fighting astride giant crocodiles.   
The source of the invaders is a towering stone obelisk that emerges from the lake, gouting water, mud, and muck. Characters brave (or greedy) enough to plumb the depths of the obelisk discover powerful magics capable of transmuting physical form, and rending the veil between shadow and light.
