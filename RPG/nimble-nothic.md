# Nimble Nothic   
## Nothic (Nimble RPG stat block)

- Name: Nothic  
- Level: 4  
- Role: Skulker / Scout

- HP: 28 (5d6 + 10)  
- Armor: 12 (natural)  
- Speed: 30 ft.

- Abilities:
  - STR: 10 (+0)
  - DEX: 14 (+2)
  - CON: 14 (+2)
  - INT: 12 (+1)
  - WIS: 13 (+1)
  - CHA: 8 (−1)

- Skills: Perception +5, Stealth +6, Investigation +4  
- Senses: Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 15  
- Languages: understands Common but speaks telepathically only to those it studies

- Traits:
  - Observant (reaction): When an enemy ends its turn within 60 ft. that the nothic can see, the nothic learns one surface trait or secret (one sentence) about that creature (GM decides), usable once per creature per short rest.
  - Creepy Gaze (special): As a bonus action, the nothic fixes its gaze on one creature within 30 ft. The target must succeed on a WIS save (DC 13) or suffer one of the following (nothic chooses): disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks until the end of its next turn, or the target takes 1d6 psychic damage and is frightened for 1 round. Usable twice, recharges on a short rest.

- Actions:
  - Multiattack: The nothic makes two attacks: one with its slam and one with its eye-ray (if available).
  - Slam: Melee, +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., 1d6 + 2 bludgeoning.
  - Eye Ray: Ranged, target within 60 ft., make a ranged attack or force WIS save (DC 13). On a failed save the target takes 2d6 psychic damage and is stunned until the end of its next turn; on success it takes half damage and is not stunned. The nothic can use Eye Ray a number of times equal to its proficiency ( +2 ) per encounter (GM may track as 2 uses), regains uses on short rest.

- Reactions:
  - Parry (reaction): Add +2 to AC against one melee attack that would hit. Must see the attacker.

- Loot/Rewards:
  - 10–30 gp, a small trinket that hints at the nothic’s studies (e.g., a preserved eye in a vial), and a 25% chance of a mysterious arcane scrap (minor useable component).

- Encounter Notes (GM):
  - Use the nothic as a lone scout in ruins or a guardian of forbidden lore. It prefers ambushes, probing victims for secrets rather than brute force. Its Observant trait is best used to reveal plot hooks or clues.
