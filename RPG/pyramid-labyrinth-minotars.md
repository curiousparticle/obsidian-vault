# Pyramid Labyrinth Minotars   
### Encounter: "The Minotaurs’ Roommate Drama"   
**Location:** A 30-foot-square room stuffed with **straw, half-eaten kebabs, and a suspiciously lumpy "bed"** (actually a pile of looted tapestries and gold coins). The air reeks of **sweat, cheap perfume, and something that might be cheese**. A **heavy wooden trapdoor** hangs open in the ceiling, revealing a shaft up to the statue room above. **Q’wacksum** is currently sprawled on the straw, having fallen through the shaft (and possibly stolen a minotaur’s snack).   
**Minotaurs Present:** 3 **hulking, bull-headed brutes** (use Dragonbane’s **Minotaur** stats, but give them **terrible fashion sense** and **overdramatic personalities**). They’re **not evil**—just **territorial, lazy, and convinced this room is their "shared apartment."**   
 --- 
### 1. The Door Bursts Open!   
As the door flies open, a **minotaur in a stained toga** (let’s call him **Bullstrom**) stomps in, **battleaxe slung over his shoulder like a towel**. He freezes, **nose wrinkling**, as he spots Q’wacksum on his "bed."   
> Bullstrom:"WHAT. IS. THIS?!" (dramatic gasp) "Mortals! In my BED! Do you know how many times I’ve told my roommates NOT to invite guests?! Last time, some fool thief tried to ‘borrow’ my axe. I had to chase him THREE LEVELS!"   
> (He points at Q’wacksum.) "And YOU—you’re the one who stole my lunch yesterday, aren’t you? I KNEW it was a duck!"   

**Roleplay Hooks:**   
- **Q’wacksum (Greedy):***"Uh… no? Maybe? That was a REALLY good kebab."* (Roll CHA to bluff or DEX to hide the crumbs.)   
- **Kipper (Kleptomaniac):***"I can fix this!"* (Proceeds to "borrow" a minotaur’s loincloth as a peace offering.)   
- **Joruna (Foolhardy):***"I’ll wrestle him for the bed!"* (Roll STR. Win = minotaurs respect her. Lose = thrown into the straw pile.)   
- **Finnian (Gluttonous):***"Wait, you have FOOD here?!"* (Dives for the kebabs. 50% chance they’re spoiled.)   
 --- 
   
### 2. Meet the Roommates   
The other two minotaurs **lumber in**, equally outraged:   
- **Gorumbo**: A **minotaur in a chef’s apron**, clutching a **burnt loaf of bread**. *"I was BAKING! Do you know how hard it is to find yeast in a PYRAMID?!"*   
- **Snortrik**: A **minotaur with a monocle**, reading a scroll titled *"How to Win Friends and Influence Mummies."\*\*"This is a VIOLATION of our lease agreement!"*   
   
**Twist:** They’re **not actually evil**—just **dramatic and territorial**. They’ll **fight if provoked**, but they’re also:   
- **Gullible** (Kipper can talk them into almost anything with a good CHA roll).   
- **Lazy** (Finnian can bribe them with food).   
- **Vain** (Joruna can flatter them into complacency).   
- **Greedy** (Q’wacksum can negotiate for the 4,500 cp under the straw… if he shares his "snacks.")   
 --- 
   
### 3. The Straw Pile Incident   
The minotaurs **demand Q’wacksum leave the bed**, but the straw pile is **hiding something**:   
- **4,500 cp** (scattered like a treasure hoarder’s nightmare).   
- **A half-buried "No Minotaurs Allowed" sign** (from a failed eviction notice).   
- **A trapdoor lever** (hidden under a kebab wrapper).   
   
**Complications:**   
- **Bullstrom’s "Bed"**: He **insists** Q’wacksum vacate immediately. Options:   
    - **Fight** (minotaurs have +2 to hit with battleaxes but -1 to initiative—they’re **not morning people**).   
    - **Negotiate** (CHA rolls to offer alternatives: "I’ll clean the room!" / "I’ll find you better snacks!").   
    - **Distract** (Finnian can "accidentally" set the kebabs on fire. Gorumbo will **panic**).   
- **The Trapdoor**: If opened **without warning**, it **dumps straw (and Q’wacksum) onto the statue room above** (1d6 damage, plus embarrassment).   
 --- 
   
### 4. The Shaft Escape (or Disaster)   
If the party tries to **climb the shaft**:   
- **Gorumbo** offers to "help" by **throwing them upward**. (Roll DEX or take 1d4 damage from the toss.)   
- **Snortrik** insists on **reading the lease agreement aloud** during the climb. (Adds +2 to the time taken.)   
- **Bullstrom** tries to **follow them up**… and gets stuck. *"I KNEW this shaft was too narrow! Curse you, pyramid architects!"*   
   
**If they fight:**   
- Minotaurs **trip over each other** (roll 1d6: 1-2 = a minotaur slips on a kebab, 3-4 = they argue over tactics, 5-6 = one tries to **intimidate by flexing** and pulls a muscle).   
- **Victory Condition**: Minotaurs **surrender if outnumbered or bribed with food/shiny objects**.   
 --- 
   
### 5. Roleplay Rewards   
Offer **WP recovery** or **bonuses** for:   
- **Q’wacksum**: If he **admits to stealing the kebab** (indulging greed), Bullstrom **respects his honesty** and shares a "secret stash" (100 cp).   
- **Kipper**: If he **steals something ridiculous** (e.g., a minotaur’s "favorite rock"), the minotaurs **chase him out laughing** (no combat, but he’s -1 to rolls until he "returns" it).   
- **Joruna**: If she **wrestles a minotaur**, she gains the **"Minotaur-Wrestler"** title (NPCs react with awe or fear).   
- **Finnian**: If he **eats the questionable kebabs**, he gains **"Iron Stomach"** (+1 to CON rolls vs. food poisoning)… or **hallucinates minotaurs singing show tunes** for 1 hour.   
 --- 
   
### 6. The Aftermath   
**If the party befriends them:**   
- Minotaurs **offer to "show them around"** (badly). *"The treasure room? Oh, it’s just past the—WAIT, IS THAT A TRAP?!"*   
- They **reveal a secret**: The shaft leads to a **statue with a gemstone eye** (clue to the Star Gem).   
   
**If the party fights them:**   
- The last minotaur **flees**, shouting *"I’M TELLING MUM!"* (foreshadowing a **minotaur matriarch** in a deeper chamber).   
- The room is **trashed**, but they find a **map to the Oasis** (drawn on a napkin).   
   
**If they escape up the shaft:**   
- The minotaurs **wave goodbye**. *"Come back anytime! But bring snacks!"*   
- The trapdoor **slams shut behind them** (DEX check to avoid 1d4 damage).   
 --- 
   
### Why This Works   
- **Madcap Tone**: Minotaurs as **bickering roommates** turns combat into comedy.   
- **Flaw Integration**: Each PC’s quirks **create solutions or chaos**.   
- **Low-Stakes Fun**: No TPK risk, just **embarrassment, minor injuries, and new rumors**.   
- **Lore Drops**: Hints about the **Star Gem, pyramid traps, or Oasis** can be hidden in the minotaurs’ ramblings.
