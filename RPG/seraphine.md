# Seraphine   
Here’s **Lady Seraphine of the Dawn** and her infamous **"Dawnbringer"** sword, fully statted for **Dragonbane** and packed with roleplay hooks, flaws, and comedic potential. This version keeps her **high-intensity, overconfident, and tragically in over her head**, while making her a fun (if exasperating) ally for your players.   
 ---

*"By the light of the dawn, I shall smite thee! ...Wait, why is my sword not glowing?!"*

**Weapons:**   
- **Dawnbringer** (see below): +1 vs. undead (but she *thinks* it’s +3).   
- **Silver Dagger** (hidden in her boot): Forgot she had it.   
   
**Skills:**   
- **Swordplay (Adept)**: She’s *technically* skilled, but her **overconfidence** leads to **wild swings**.   
- **Divine Lore (Novice)**: Knows *just enough* about undead to be dangerous.   
- **Persuasion (Adept)**: *"Trust me, I’m a paladin!"* (Works 60% of the time.)   
- **Bluff (Expert)**: "This sword is **legendary*!"* (It’s not.)   
   
**Flaws:**   
1. **Overconfident**: *"I’ve got this!"* (She doesn’t.)   
2. **Dramatic**: *"WITNESS THE POWER OF DAWN!"* (Trips.)   
3. **Secretly Terrified**: Whispers *"I’m not ready for this"* when alone.   
4. **Equipment Snob**: Refuses to use "peasant weapons" (like the dagger in her boot).   
   
**Gear:**   
- **Fake Holy Symbol**: A gilded locket (bought from a merchant; 10% chance it *actually* glows vs. undead).   
- **Embroidered Cloak**: "The sunburst pattern intimidates my foes!" (It doesn’t.)   
- **Journal**: Full of **dramatic sketches** of her "heroic deeds" (mostly exaggerations).

### Dawnbringer: The "Legendary" Sword   
"Forged in the fires of dawn itself! ...Okay, fine, I bought it from a merchant in Bralizzar, but it **looks* magical!"*

|:-------------------|:-------------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------|
|  **Damage**   <br> |   1d8   <br> |                       +1 vs. undead (she *believes* it’s +3).   <br> |
| **Special**   <br> |    --   <br> |                        **Glows faintly** in darkness (paint).   <br> |
|    **Flaw**   <br> |    --   <br> |                     **Fades in 1d4 hours** (paint wears off).   <br> |
|  **Secret**   <br> |    --   <br> | **Actually a family heirloom** (but she pawned the real one).   <br> |

**Abilities:**   
- **"Radiant Strike"**: Once per fight, she can **declare a dramatic attack** (e.g., "DAWN’S JUDGMENT!"). Roll **Style** (CHA). On success, +1 damage *and* enemies flinch (lose their next action). On failure, she **trips** (1d4 damage to herself).   
- **"Divine Aegis"**: If she **shouts a prayer**, allies gain +1 to their next roll (but she **loses 1 WP** from the effort).   
   
**Roleplay Hooks:**   
- **The Paint Job**: The "glow" is **luminescent paint** (from a merchant’s "magic" stall). It **smudges** if she sweats.   
- **The Real Dawnbringer**: The *actual* heirloom was sold to pay for her "training." If the party finds it, she’ll **break down** ("I’m a fraud!").   
- **Ghouls Hate It**: They **hiss and recoil**—not from magic, but because it’s **tacky**.   
 --- 
   
### Seraphine’s Combat Tactics   
1. **Taunt First**: *"Come, fiends! Taste steel!"* (Enemies get +1 to hit her if they’re smart.)   
2. **Wild Swings**: Rolls **Style (CHA)** for flashy moves. Success = +1 damage. Failure = **hits a pillar** (1d4 damage to herself).   
3. **Retreat? Never!**: She’ll **fight to the last HP**, then **collapse dramatically**.   
4. **Teamwork?**: *"Stand back, I’ve got this!"* (She doesn’t. But if a PC **orders her to retreat**, she’ll sulk but obey.)   
 --- 
   
### Roleplaying Seraphine   
### Quirks & Dialogue   
- **On Undead**: *"Disgusting creatures! The light of dawn shall PURGE THEE!"* (Ghouls laugh.)   
- **On Her Sword**: *"This blade has felled a hundred foes!"* (It hasn’t.)   
- **On Retreat**: "Strategic… **repositioning*!"* (She’s bleeding.)   
- **On the Party**: *"Finally, worthy allies! Now, watch and learn—"* (Trips.)   
   
### Secrets to Reveal   
1. **Her "Training"**: She **bought a fake knighthood** from a scammer in Bralizzar.   
2. **The Spirit’s Lie**: The "desert spirit" who sent her was **Munafik in disguise**.   
3. **Her Fear of Failure**: If a PC **comforts her**, she’ll admit: *"I just wanted to be a hero like in the stories…"*   
 --- 
   
### Seraphine’s Knowledge (If Pressed)   
She’ll **monologue** if given the chance (or if the party **pins her down**):   
- **Munafik’s Heart**: "It’s not in his body! He laughed when I struck him—said his ‘life was too precious to carry.’ Then he flew into the dome like a **coward*!"*   
- **The Ghouls**: "They were priests once. They **chose* this fate! Fools…"* (She’s **terrified** they’ll turn her.)   
- **The Waterfall**: *"There’s a chamber with a waterfall—he vanished there. But the way is trapped…"* (She **doesn’t know how** to disarm them.)   
- **The High Priest**: *"He called himself ‘Munafik.’ Said he was ‘second only to Osiris.’"* (Shudders.) *"His voice… it was like honey and rot."*   
 --- 
   
### Encounter Flow: "The Paladin’s Last Stand"   
1. **Entrance**: Party finds her **surrounded by ghouls**, **taunting them** while **bleeding**.   
2. **Ghoul Tactics**: They **toy with her**, waiting for her to tire. One **hisses**: "Little light… so bright… so **fragile*."*   
3. **Seraphine’s Mistakes**:   
    - **Round 1**: She **swings wildly**, hits a pillar. *"Curses!"*   
    - **Round 2**: She **tries "Radiant Strike"**—rolls a fail, **trips into the ghouls**.   
    - **Round 3**: She **panics**, shouts for help. *"AID ME, NOBLE WARRIORS!"*   
4. **PC Options**:   
    - **Fight Smart**: Use **fire, holy symbols, or traps** (ghouls fear fire).   
    - **Negotiate**: One ghoul **was a priest**—persuade it to betray the others (CHA/WIL roll).   
    - **Retreat**: Seraphine **won’t go willingly**—someone must **carry her**.   
5. **Aftermath**:   
    - **If Saved**: She **joins the party**, but **insists on leading** (badly).   
    - **If Left**: She **dies dramatically**, but her sword **flares**, revealing a **hidden passage**.   
    - **If Turned**: Reappears later as a **ghoul in her armor**, hissing: *"You… left me…"*

### Why This Works for Your Game   
- **High-Intensity Combat**: Ghouls are **deadly but outsmartable**, and Seraphine’s **flaws create urgency**.   
- **Roleplay Gold**: Her **ego, insecurity, and fake sword** give every PC a chance to **clash, comfort, or exploit her**.   
- **Lore Delivery**: She **knows critical info** but **won’t shut up**, forcing players to **extract it mid-chaos**.   
- **Consequences**: Her **mark by Munafik** means **future encounters** with wraiths/ghouls will **target her first**.   
 --- 
   
### Follow-Up Hooks   
1. **Munafik’s Taunt**: If saved, his voice echoes: *"Ahhh, the little hero lives… for now."* (The party now has a **time limit**—he’s coming.)   
2. **The Fake Sword**: If exposed, she **breaks down**: *"I just… wanted to be like the paladins in the stories…"* (WP recovery for comforting her.)   
3. **Ghoul Betrayal**: The "less feral" ghoul **follows the party**, whispering: *"I know where the heart is… but the price is high…"*
