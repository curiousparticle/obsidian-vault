# Paladin Fight   
Here’s your **revised, high-intensity encounter** with the **Female Paladin**, now reimagined as a **showy, overconfident warrior** who’s clearly in over her head—but too proud to admit it. This version keeps the **challenge and urgency** of the original while adding **dramatic tension, roleplay hooks, and opportunities for the players to shine (or flounder)**.   
 --- 
### Encounter: "The Paladin’s Last Stand"   
**Location:** An octagonal chamber, **30 feet across**, lit by flickering torchlight. The air smells of **burning incense and old blood**. At the center, a **female paladin** stands surrounded by **eight ghouls**, her **sword flashing in dramatic arcs** as she shouts taunts at her enemies. The ghouls **hiss and snarl**, but they seem **hesitant to close in**—almost as if they’re **toying with her**.   
**The Paladin: Lady Seraphine of the Dawn**   
- **Appearance:** A **tall, armored woman** in **polished silver plate**, her **cloak embroidered with a sunburst**. Her **sword glows faintly** (a minor magic effect, but she acts like it’s legendary).   
- **Personality:**  **Bravado over brains**. She’s **loud, dramatic, and convinced she’s the hero of this story**—but she’s **clearly exhausted**, her breaths coming in ragged gasps. She’s **wounded** (half HP) and **outnumbered**, but she **won’t stop monologuing**.   
- **Stats:** Use the **Paladin stats** from Dragonbane, but tweak her **HP to 12/24** (she’s been fighting a while). Her **sword is +1 vs. undead**, but she’s **low on Willpower Points (WP)**.   
 --- 
   
### 1. The Dramatic Entrance   
As the party enters, **Lady Seraphine** is mid-taunt, **spinning her sword in a flourish** that nearly knocks over a torch.   
> Seraphine:"Come then, abominations! Let’s see if your teeth are as sharp as your master’s TONGUE!" (She lunges at a ghoul, but it dodges easily, and she trips over her own cloak.)   
> Ghoul:"Hissss… foolish meat…" (They circle closer.)   

**First Impressions:**   
- **Joruna (Foolhardy):***"Finally, someone who fights like me!"* (Wants to charge in.)   
- **Kipper (Kleptomaniac):***"That cloak looks expensive…"* (Eyeing her gear.)   
- **Finnian (Gluttonous):***"She’s gonna get us all killed… but I admire the style."* (Already snacking on rations.)   
- **Q’wacksum (Greedy):***"If we save her, can we sell her sword?"* (Whispering to Kipper.)   
 --- 
   
### 2. The Ghouls’ Game   
The ghouls **aren’t attacking in earnest**—they’re **herding her**, waiting for her to tire out. They **hiss and laugh**, exchanging glances like they’re **sharing a joke**.   
**Twist:** They’re **stalling for time**. If the party doesn’t act, **Munafik’s wraiths** (or more ghouls) will arrive in **1d4 rounds**.   
**Tactics:**   
- Ghouls **focus on Seraphine**, but if a PC attacks, they’ll **turn on the easiest target** (likely Finnian or Q’wacksum).   
- **Seraphine’s "Strategy":** She **taunts, postures, and uses flashy but ineffective moves** (e.g., trying to **reflect torchlight into their eyes**). She’s **not actually bad**—just **overconfident and exhausted**.   
   
**Roleplay Hooks:**   
- **Seraphine’s Ego:** She **refuses help** at first. *"I’ve got this! Watch and learn, rookies!"*   
    - **Persuasion Options:**   
        - **Flatter her** (CHA roll): *"Your technique is inspiring! Let us assist you in this glorious battle!"*   
        - **Insult her** (CHA roll, harder): *"You’re gonna die if you don’t shut up and let us help."*   
        - **Distract the ghouls** (DEX/AGL roll): Throw food, break a torch, etc.   
- **Her Weakness:** She’s **terrified of failure** (but won’t admit it). If a PC **calls her out**, she’ll **lash out**—then **break down** if pressed.   
 --- 
   
### 3. The Truth Comes Out   
If the party **stabilizes the fight** (or Seraphine is reduced to 2 HP), she **finally admits the truth** between gasps:   
> Seraphine:"Fine! YES, I’m in over my head! That spirit I met in the desert? It lied! Said the High Priest’s heart was here! But it’s not—it’s by the waterfall! And his magic… it’s not like anything I’ve seen. He just… laughed when I hit him. Like I was a child swinging a stick!" (She clutches her side, wincing.) "And now my sword’s glow is fading…"   

**Lore Dump (If Pressed):**   
- The **High Priest (Munafik)** is **undeath incarnate**—his heart is hidden elsewhere (see **Dome of Flight**).   
- The **ghouls were once priests** who **turned to dark magic** for eternal life. *"They’re not mindless! They’re laughing at me!"*   
- She **cornered Munafik by the waterfall**, but he **flew into the dome** and vanished. *"He said something about ‘the heart not being with him’…"*   
   
**Complications:**   
- Her **sword’s magic is fading** (loses its +1 bonus in 1d4 hours).   
- She’s **marked by Munafik’s magic** (ghouls/wraiths will **pursue her relentlessly**).   
- She **doesn’t know the way out**—but she **does know the path to the waterfall**.   
 --- 
   
### 4. The Ghouls’ Last Gambit   
If the fight drags on, the ghouls **change tactics**:   
- **One ghoul** grabs Seraphine’s cloak and **yanks her into their circle**.   
- **Another** hisses: *"Meat… offers itself…"* (They’re **debating whether to eat her or turn her**.)   
- **Seraphine panics**: *"DO SOMETHING, YOU IDIOTS!"* (She **kicks a ghoul in the face**, but it **laughs**.)   
   
**Options for the Party:**   
- **Fight Smart:** Use **fire, holy symbols, or traps** (ghouls are weak to fire and **fear divine light**).   
- **Negotiate?** One ghoul **seems less feral** (it was a priest once). A **CHA or WIL roll** might reveal it **hates Munafik** and will **betray the others** if promised freedom.   
- **Retreat:** Seraphine **won’t go willingly**—she’d rather die than "abandon her quest." Someone must **drag her**.   
 --- 
   
### 5. Aftermath: The Paladin’s Pride   
**If Saved:**   
- She **grudgingly thanks the party**, but **insists she’d have won eventually**.   
- She **joins them** (if no evil alignments), but:   
    - **Demands to lead** (bad idea).   
    - **Constantly "advises"** (usually wrong).   
    - **Tries to "protect" the weakest PC** (likely Finnian or Q’wacksum) by **putting them in danger**.   
- **Bonus:** If a PC **calls her out on her BS**, she’ll **sulk for an hour**, then **admit they’re right** (and gain +1 WP).   
   
**If Left Behind:**   
- She **dies dramatically**, but her **sword’s magic flares one last time**, revealing a **hidden passage** (to the waterfall).   
- The ghouls **mock her corpse**: *"Foolish meat… thought it was a hero…"* (Cue **Munafik’s laughter** echoing from above.)   
   
**If the Party Fails:**   
- The ghouls **turn her into one of them**. She **reappears later** as a **ghoul in fancy armor**, hissing: *"You… left me…"* (Now she **hunts them**.)   
 --- 
   
### 6. Roleplaying Seraphine   
**Key Traits:**   
- **Overconfident:***"I’ve fought worse than this in my sleep!"* (She hasn’t.)   
- **Dramatic:***"For the light of DAWN!"* (Trips over her own feet.)   
- **Secretly Terrified:** If alone with a PC, she’ll **whisper**: *"I don’t think I’m strong enough for this…"*   
- **Loyal (Stupidly):** Once she joins, she’ll **take a hit for a PC**—then **complain about it for hours**.   
   
**Quirks:**   
- **Names her sword** ("Dawnbringer"). It’s **not actually magic**—she **painted it with glow-in-the-dark paint**.   
- **Hates undead** (but is **terrified of them**).   
- **Carries a "holy symbol"** (it’s a **fake**, bought from a merchant).   
 --- 
   
### Why This Works   
- **High-Intensity Combat:** Ghouls are **deadly but outsmartable**, and Seraphine’s **flaws create urgency**.   
- **Roleplay Gold:** Her **ego and insecurity** give every PC a chance to **shine or clash** with her.   
- **Lore Delivery:** She **knows critical info** but **won’t shut up**, forcing players to **extract it mid-chaos**.   
- **Consequences:** Her **mark by Munafik** means **future encounters** with wraiths/ghouls will **target her first**.   
 --- 
   
### Example Dialogue   
> Seraphine:"Ah, reinforcements! Excellent timing—I was just about to finish these fiends off!" (A ghoul bites her arm. She yelsps.)   
> Joruna:"You’re bleeding!"   
> Seraphine:"It’s just a scratch! Watch this!" (She swings wildly, hits a pillar. The ghouls laugh.)   
> Kipper:"I could distract them… if you give me your cloak."   
> Seraphine:"MY CLOAK?! It’s EMBROIDERED!"   
> Finnian:"I’ve got a better idea—" (Throws a kebab. Ghouls pause. Seraphine: "…Why do you have food?!")   

 --- 
### Follow-Up Hooks   
1. **Munafik’s Taunt:** If Seraphine is saved, **Munafik’s voice echoes** from the dome above: *"Ahhh, the little hero lives… for now."* (The party now has a **time limit**—he’s coming.)   
2. **The Fake Sword:** If exposed, Seraphine **breaks down**: *"I just… wanted to be like the paladins in the stories…"* (WP recovery for comforting her.)   
3. **Ghoul Betrayal:** The "less feral" ghoul **follows the party**, whispering: *"I know where the heart is… but the price is high…"*
