# Final Battle out of Hell   
Here’s a **step-by-step breakdown** of your climactic encounter, designed for **Dragonbane** and tailored to your party’s dynamics. This encounter is **action-packed, morally urgent, and cinematic**, with clear stakes, tough choices, and a dramatic twist at the end.   
 --- 
## Encounter: Escape from the Citadel of Hell   
### 1. The Weakened Zurboh   
**Description:**
As the party frees Zurboh from the cage, he collapses to his knees, his body wracked with pain. His skin is burned, his voice a ragged whisper. He clutches a **shattered rune stone**—the remnants of his failed escape attempt. The doppelgänger’s laughter echoes as the citadel trembles.   
**Zurboh’s State:**   
- **Weakened:** Zurboh is at **0 Willpower** and **half Health**. He can barely stand, let alone fight.   
- **Incantation:** He gasps, *"The way out… I can open it… but I need time. Get me to the top of the citadel. The demon’s name… it’s the key…"*   
- **Mechanics:** Zurboh will take **3 rounds** to complete the incantation once at the top. The party must protect him and buy time.   
 --- 
   
### 2. The Ascent: A Chase Through the Citadel   
**Description:**
The citadel is a **crumbling, labyrinthine tower**, its stairs and bridges collapsing as the party climbs. Behind them, the **doppelgänger** (now revealed as a **massive, multi-limbed horror**) pursues, taunting them with their own voices. The walls are lined with **screaming souls**, some reaching out for help, others trying to drag the party into the abyss.   
**Chase Mechanics:**   
- **Skill Challenge:** The party must **navigate obstacles** while carrying/following Zurboh. Use a mix of:   
    - **Athletics (STR/DEX):** Leaping over collapsing stairs, dodging falling debris.   
    - **Willpower (WIL):** Resisting the whispers of the damned or the doppelgänger’s taunts.   
    - **Perception (INT):** Spotting safe paths or hidden traps.   
- **Failure Consequences:**   
    - A character falls behind and must **fight off a lesser demon** (use a **Demon Spawn** stat block).   
    - Zurboh is **grabbed by a spectral hand** and must be freed (costs an action).   
- **Doppelgänger’s Taunts:**   
    - To Rook: *"You left Kael to die. Who will you sacrifice next?"*   
    - To Dong: *"Your clan fell because of you. Will your friends be next?"*   
    - To Juni: *"You’re no druid. You’re just a beast in human skin."*   
    - To Trixie: *"Your family starved. How many more will suffer for your greed?"*   
   
**Twist:**
Halfway up, the party finds a **collapsed bridge**. They must:   
- **Repair it** (requires a toolkit and a **Hack and Slash roll**).   
- **Swing across** using chains (Athletics, risk of falling).   
- **Carry Zurboh across** (teamwork check).   
 --- 
   
### 3. The Final Fight: At the Citadel’s Peak   
**Description:**
The party reaches the **rooftop of the citadel**, a circular platform wreathed in hellfire. Zurboh begins his incantation, but the **doppelgänger bursts through the floor**, now a **towering, multi-faced abomination** (use a **Greater Demon** stat block with legendary actions). The sky is a swirling vortex—the **exit from hell**—but it’s unstable. Zurboh warns: *"If I’m interrupted, the portal collapses!"*   
**Doppelgänger’s Abilities:**   
- **Multiattack:** Claws (2d8 damage) and a **soul-rending scream** (Willpower save or lose 1d4 Willpower).   
- **Mirror of Flaws:** Once per round, it **mimics a player’s signature move** (e.g., if Rook uses *Backstab*, the doppelgänger does too).   
- **Summon Minions:** Every 2 rounds, it summons **2 Demon Spawns** from the abyss below.   
   
**Zurboh’s Incantation:**   
- **3 Rounds to Complete:** Each round, Zurboh rolls a **d6**:   
    - **1-2:** The incantation **falter**. The doppelgänger gains a **free attack** on Zurboh.   
    - **3-4:** The incantation **holds**. The party gains **+1 forward** on their next attack.   
    - **5-6:** The incantation **strengthens**. The doppelgänger is **distracted** (loses its next action).   
- **If Zurboh is attacked:** The incantation is **interrupted**. The party must **stabilize him** (Heal check) to restart it.   
   
**Environmental Hazards:**   
- **Hellfire Geysers:** Erupt randomly (DEX save or take 1d6 fire damage).   
- **Screaming Souls:** Can be **freed** (Willpower check) to **distract the doppelgänger** for 1 round.   
 --- 
   
### 4. The Escape (or Betrayal)   
**Success:**
If the party **protects Zurboh for 3 rounds**, the portal **explodes open**, and the party is **sucked back into the mortal world**. They materialize in **Farfall’s ruins**, gasping for air.   
**Failure:**
If Zurboh is **killed or the incantation fails**, the portal **implodes**, and the party is **scattered across hell**. They must **find each other** and escape another way (future quest hook).   
 --- 
### 5. The Devil’s Bargain   
**Description:**
As the party stumbles into Farfall, a **massive, clawed hand** erupts from the ground, **gripping Zurboh by the throat**. A **horned devil** (use a **Devil Lord** stat block) pulls him halfway back into hell, laughing.   
**Devil’s Dialogue:**
"Did you really think he was yours to keep? Zurboh’s soul was ***sold long ago**. But…"* (it grins) "…he left you a ***parting gift**."*   
**Zurboh’s Last Act:**
As he’s dragged into hell, Zurboh **slams the shattered rune stone into the ground**. It **explodes into five fragments**, scattering across Farfall. He screams:
"Find the pieces… speak the name… or ***Farfall burns**!"*   
**The Puzzle Begins:**
The party now has a **race against time** to recover the fragments and **learn the demon’s true name**—or watch Farfall fall to the demon’s onslaught.   
 --- 
## Why This Works for Your Game   
- **Action-Packed:** The chase and final fight are **cinematic and urgent**, with clear stakes.   
- **Moral Choices:** The party must **protect Zurboh at all costs**, even if it means sacrificing their own safety.   
- **Consequences:** Failure means **being trapped in hell** or **losing Zurboh forever**.   
- **Sets Up the Next Arc:** The **scattered rune fragments** become the **next major quest**, tying into your **Demonbane** theme.
