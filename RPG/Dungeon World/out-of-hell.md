# Out of hell   
Given your preference for **action-driven quests** (rather than introspective truths) and your **Dragonbane** setting, here are three quests tied to the **shattered rune stone puzzle**. Each quest requires the party to **act, investigate, or overcome a challenge** in the world—no deep confessions required. I’ve drawn inspiration from your past notes (e.g., Juni’s connection to nature, Dong’s leadership, Trixie’s cunning, and Rook’s betrayal themes) to make them feel organic.   
 --- 
### Quest 1: The Rune of the Forgotten Beast   
**Fragment’s Clue:**
*"The first piece hums with the voice of a beast that remembers what the world has forgotten."*   
**Quest:**
The fragment leads the party to **Greber**, Juni’s hometown. The villagers speak of a **"Ghost Bear"**—a spectral creature that has been seen wandering the outskirts of the village at night, howling mournfully. The bear is Juni’s **ancestor**, a druid who bound her spirit to the land to protect it. But the demon’s influence has corrupted the spirit, turning it restless.   
**What the Party Must Do:**   
- **Track the Ghost Bear** through the forest, following clues (e.g., claw marks, disturbed shrines).   
- **Confront the spirit** in a ritual combat (not to kill, but to calm it). Juni must use her druidic magic to **communicate with the bear**, while the others defend her from the demonic shadows clinging to it.   
- **Restore the bear’s memory** by placing the rune fragment on an ancient standing stone in the forest. This reveals the first part of the demon’s name:   
    > "Who feasts on..."   

   
**Stakes:**   
- If they fail, the Ghost Bear becomes a **harbinger of the demon**, attacking travelers and spreading fear.   
- If they succeed, the bear’s spirit grants Juni a **boon** (e.g., temporary resistance to demonic corruption).

### Quest 2: The Rune of the Broken Oath   
**Fragment’s Clue:**
*"The second piece burns with the weight of a promise shattered by a leader’s hand."*   
**Quest:**
The fragment points to **Dong’s former clan**, now scattered after his fall from grace. Rumors speak of a **cursed war banner**—a relic of Dong’s reign—stolen by a rival clan. The banner is said to **whisper lies** to those who touch it, driving them to paranoia and infighting.   
**What the Party Must Do:**   
- **Infiltrate the rival clan’s stronghold** (stealth, deception, or brute force).   
- **Retrieve the banner**, but it’s guarded by warriors who believe it’s their rightful spoil.   
- **Purify the banner** in a ritual: Dong must **publicly renounce his past tyranny** (not a confession, but a declaration of change) while the party defends him from the banner’s whispers. This stabilizes the fragment, revealing:   
    > "the bones of..."   

   
**Stakes:**   
- If they fail, the rival clan **uses the banner to rally against Dong**, forcing him to either fight his past or flee.   
- If they succeed, Dong gains a **reputation boost** among his former people, and the banner becomes a **symbol of redemption** (e.g., grants +1 to Leadership rolls).   
 --- 
   
### Quest 3: The Rune of the Stolen Secret   
**Fragment’s Clue:**
*"The third piece flickers with the shadow of a deal made in the dark, where silver changed hands for a life not worth saving."*   
**Quest:**
The fragment leads to **Greenvale’s black market**, where a **mysterious merchant** (a doppelgänger in disguise) is selling a **"Memory Vial"**—a glass orb containing a stolen memory. The memory belongs to **Rook**, from the night he betrayed The Scoun. The doppelgänger is using it to **blackmail Rook**, threatening to reveal his past to the party.   
**What the Party Must Do:**   
- **Track the merchant** through Greenvale’s underbelly (gathering info, chasing leads).   
- **Confront the doppelgänger** in a tense negotiation or fight. The vial is fragile—if broken, the memory is lost forever.   
- **Return the memory to Rook**, but he must **choose**: destroy it (losing a piece of himself) or keep it (risking future blackmail). Either choice stabilizes the fragment, revealing:   
    > "the forgotten..."   

   
**Stakes:**   
- If they fail, the doppelgänger **escalates the blackmail**, forcing Rook to make a dangerous bargain.   
- If they succeed, Rook gains a **moment of clarity** (e.g., temporary advantage on Willpower rolls).   
 --- 
   
### Quest 4: The Rune of the Hollow Crown   
**Fragment’s Clue:**
*"The fourth piece echoes with the laughter of a king who ruled nothing but ash."*   
**Quest:**
The fragment points to the **ruins of a fallen noble’s estate** near Farfall. The estate is haunted by the ghost of **Lord Uforn’s steward**, a man who helped summon the demon and was **erased from history** as punishment. The steward’s spirit is bound to a **cursed crown**, which he offers to the party in exchange for his name being remembered.   
**What the Party Must Do:**   
- **Navigate the ruins**, avoiding traps and spectral guardians.   
- **Recover the steward’s name** from a hidden ledger in the estate’s vault.   
- **Speak his name aloud** in the throne room, freeing his spirit. The crown crumbles, revealing the fragment’s secret:   
    > "and drinks..."   

   
**Stakes:**   
- If they fail, the steward’s spirit **possesses one of them**, whispering secrets to the demon.   
- If they succeed, the party gains a **favor from Farfall’s nobles** (e.g., access to restricted knowledge).   
 --- 
   
### Quest 5: The Rune of the Guilty Coin   
**Fragment’s Clue:**
*"The fifth piece weeps with the weight of a debt unpaid, where gold bought silence and blood bought time."*   
**Quest:**
The fragment leads to **Trixie’s cousin in Greber**, who is being extorted by a **corrupt magistrate**. The magistrate holds a **bloodstained coin**—payment for covering up a crime tied to the demon’s summoning. The coin is cursed: anyone who carries it **hears the whispers of the guilty**.   
**What the Party Must Do:**   
- **Investigate the magistrate’s crimes** (gather evidence, intimidate witnesses).   
- **Steal the coin** from his heavily guarded manor.   
- **Return it to the earth** (bury it in Greber’s sacred grove), lifting the curse and revealing:   
    > "the tears of the guilty."   

   
**Stakes:**   
- If they fail, the magistrate **uses the coin to frame the party**, turning the village against them.   
- If they succeed, Trixie’s cousin is freed, and the party gains a **contact in Greber’s underground**.   
 --- 
   
### Putting It All Together   
- **Reward:** When all five fragments are stabilized, the demon’s true name is revealed:   
    > "Vorthas, Who Feasts on the Bones of the Forgotten and Drinks the Tears of the Guilty."   

- **Mechanical Hook:** Speaking the name **weakens the demon** in the final battle (e.g., reduces its armor or grants the party a bonus).   
- **Thematic Tie:** Each quest reinforces your party’s strengths—**Juni’s connection to nature, Dong’s leadership, Trixie’s cunning, and Rook’s redemption**—without requiring emotional confessions.
