1. **Trixie's Rescue Mission**: Trixie, with the aid of the Wasp-Zuni creature, saves

1. **The Deputy High Wizard's Concerns**: The Deputy High Wizard, Zurboh, is troubled

1. **Demon's Challenge**: A confrontation ensues with Uforn, a powerful demon who   
   
challenges Zurboh over the gem's transformation. The gem's soulless state is attributed   
to potential alchemical activities in Greenvale.   
1. **Escape from Demonic Pursuit**: After being forced to flee due to Uforn's forces,   
   
the group narrowly escapes injuries and resumes their journey with renewed resolve.
