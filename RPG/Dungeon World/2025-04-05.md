# 2025-04-05   
Uforn tells the group   
- Tricked by the demon lord who then paralyzed him   
- He escaped but not after finding out that the DL had killed the high wizard   
- The DL now has the ear of the king, and is trying to kill off the wizards, who are the only people who can oppose him   
- Uforn, the Deputy High Wizard who is on the run after discovering chaos in Farfall caused by a demon posing as him. They learn about the use of demon-filled gems for magic power and are faced with a potential mine takeover by the King's guard, which is rumored to be influenced by a demon.   
   
The group decides to approach the Witches of Whapum for help in locating High Wizard Zurboh and dealing with the demon-related threats. They encounter resistance from the Witches initially but eventually gain their assistance after offering three coins. However, Uforn mysteriously disappears using a spell shortly after meeting the Witches.   
The Witch of Whapum provides crucial information about Zurboh's whereabouts and hints at a larger conspiracy involving demons and magic users. The group is introduced to another demon, Flabistus, trapped in a flagstone, and decides to seek out Druids in For’st for help in freeing him, believing they hold the key to resolving this complex magical dilemma.
