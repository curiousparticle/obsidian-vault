# CF - Demons Revolt   
# Dangers   
1. Demons   
2. Wizards   
   
## Demons - Open the Gates of Hell   
### Impending Doom   
Usurpation (the chain of order comes apart, someone rightful is displaced)   
The king and the nobles are displaced and the demons rule.   
### GM Moves for Planar Forces   
- Turn an organization (corrupt or infiltrate with influence)   
- Give dreams of prophecy   
- Lay a Curse on a foe   
- Extract a promise in exchange for a boon   
- Attack indirectly, through intermediaries   
- Rarely, when the stars are right, attack directly   
- Foster rivalries with other, similar powers   
- Expose someone to a Truth, wanted or otherwise   
   
## Wizards - Seek Magical Power   
### Impending Doom   
Tyranny (of the strong over the weak or the few over the many)   
The wizards continue to control the king and demons and continue to consolidate power.   
### GM Moves for Arcane Enemies   
- Learn forbidden knowledge   
- Cast a spell over time and space   
- Attack a foe with magic, directly or otherwise   
- Spy on someone with a scrying spell   
- Recruit a follower or toady   
- Tempt someone with promises   
- Demand a sacrifice   
   
## Description and Cast   
Demon's are tired of being used and abused by the wizards. The gems of binding often leave them there for decades, alone. The are often killed in battle, though they are brought back, it is horrifically painful.   
- DHW - **Uforn** - Tall and lean with an easy way about him. Well dressed, long blond hair tied back.   
- HW - **Zurbo** - Hunched over distracted with a distracted manner. Not good with social boundaries.   
   
## Grim portents   
### Demons   
1. King closes down the gem purification and outlaws their use   
2. King forces wizards out of Farfall   
3. There is a rise in dark cults in Farfall which forces out other religions   
4. Dark clouds form over the city   
5. Conjuring begins as does sacrifices   
6. The city falls into darkness and is consumed by demons
